`[CUSTOMER]` (the **controller**) and `[PROVIDER]` (the **processor**) for the
**hosted Piyaz service at `app.piyaz.ai`**. It governs the processing of personal
data that the controller places into Piyaz workspaces, under **Article 28 GDPR**.
If you self-host Piyaz, this DPA does not apply to your deployment: you are the
controller and the processor for your own instance.

## Sub-processors

The controller authorises the processor to engage the sub-processors listed on the
[sub-processor list](/subprocessors). That page names each sub-processor, its

### Changes to sub-processors

Under **Article 28(2) GDPR**, the controller receives advance notice before the
processor adds or replaces a sub-processor, so the controller can object. To
subscribe to sub-processor change notices, email **privacy@piyaz.ai** with the
subject line **"Subscribe: sub-processor changes"**. Subscribed controllers
receive at least `[NOTICE PERIOD]` days' advance notice before a new or replacement
sub-processor begins processing personal data.
